Welcome to **open2nix** — a new static site that extends my [WordPress blog](https://open2nix.wordpress.com) with a faster, version-controlled home for weekly tech posts.

## What to expect

This blog covers the tools and systems I use day to day:

- **Nix & NixOS** — reproducible builds, flakes, home-manager configs
- **Linux & homelabs** — self-hosting, containers, networking
- **Open source tooling** — CLIs, editors, automation scripts
- **DevOps & infrastructure** — GitOps, CI/CD, observability

## Why Astro?

Astro lets me write posts in plain Markdown, deploy automatically via GitHub Actions, and ship zero JavaScript by default. It's fast, simple, and fits the Unix philosophy of doing one thing well.

## Post schedule

I publish at least once a week — usually on weekends. Posts are cross-listed with the WordPress blog where they fit, but new technical deep-dives will live here first.
